\begin{frame}[fragile]{COGANT --- Supplementary Materials}
\protect\phantomsection\label{cogant-supplementary-materials}
This appendix collects the detailed artifacts that support the main
text: the full per-role roundtrip table across all 23 evaluation targets
(Appendix A), the rule-family ablation reconstructed directly from the
mapping-kind breakdown (Appendix B), a Galois-connection proof sketch
for the forward/reverse pair and the ε-isomorphism theorem (Appendix C),
the discrete-POMDP active-inference mathematics underlying the runnable
cycle reported in Section 5 (Appendix D), and an extended related-work
bibliography of \textbf{64 curated bibliography entries} in
\textbf{Appendix E} (compiled from the 83-entry annotated pool in
\href{../cogant/docs/evaluation/LITERATURE.md}{\texttt{../cogant/docs/evaluation/LITERATURE.md}}),
organized across 10 research areas.

Numerical data in Appendices A and B are sourced verbatim from
\texttt{../cogant/docs/evaluation/ROUNDTRIP\_EVAL.md},
\texttt{../cogant/docs/evaluation/REAL\_WORLD\_EVAL.md},
\texttt{../cogant/docs/evaluation/EMPIRICAL\_CLAIM.md}, and
\texttt{../cogant/docs/evaluation/CONSTRAINT\_FIX.md}. Where the same
measurement appears in more than one source file the value in
\texttt{../cogant/docs/evaluation/ROUNDTRIP\_EVAL.md} (the canonical
roundtrip artefact, post wave 14) takes precedence.
\end{frame}
