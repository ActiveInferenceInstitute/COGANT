\begin{frame}[fragile]{Appendix A --- Full Roundtrip ε Table (per-role
breakdown)}
\protect\phantomsection\label{appendix-a-full-roundtrip-ux3b5-table-per-role-breakdown}
The ε metric used throughout the paper is the
\texttt{role\_match\_score} returned by
\texttt{cogant.reverse.idempotency.compute\_isomorphism\_report(orig\_gnn,\ synth\_gnn)}.
It is a multiset similarity over the role populations of the forward GNN
(\texttt{orig\_gnn}) and the re-forwarded synthesized package
(\texttt{synth\_gnn}). In this appendix we further decompose ε into four
per-role components ε\_HIDDEN\_STATE, ε\_OBSERVATION, ε\_ACTION, and
ε\_CONSTRAINT, each computed as the multiset-similarity restricted to a
single role category:

\begin{quote}
ε\_role(P) = min(count\_orig(role), count\_synth(role)) /
max(count\_orig(role), count\_synth(role))
\end{quote}

with the convention ε\_role = 1.0 when both counts are zero (the role is
vacuously preserved) and ε\_role = 0.0 when exactly one of the two
counts is zero (the role has been introduced or dropped). The overall ε
reported by \texttt{compute\_isomorphism\_report} is the mean of the
per-role components over the roles present in at least one side, which
matches the values reported in
\texttt{../cogant/docs/evaluation/ROUNDTRIP\_EVAL.md} and reproduced in
the final column.

\begin{block}{A.2 Pre-fix vs post-fix for affected repositories (wave 14
CONSTRAINT fix)}
\protect\phantomsection\label{a.2-pre-fix-vs-post-fix-for-affected-repositories-wave-14-constraint-fix}
The wave‑14 CONSTRAINT synthesizer fix
(\texttt{../cogant/docs/evaluation/CONSTRAINT\_FIX.md}) strips the
planner \texttt{cnst\_} prefix from synthesized constraint function
names and emits \texttt{check\_*} functions instead, so that the forward
pipeline's \texttt{PreferenceRule} detects them as CONSTRAINT nodes (the
rule matches on
\texttt{check\textbar{}test\_\textbar{}assert\_\textbar{}validate} in
the function name). Before the fix, every synthesized constraint stub
was silently dropped from the synthesized role multiset; after the fix,
exactly one \texttt{check\_*} stub is emitted per \texttt{NodePlan} in
\texttt{plan.constraint\_checks}, so origin and synth CONSTRAINT counts
match exactly for the affected repositories.

\begin{block}{A.3 POMDP shape match across all 23 targets}
\protect\phantomsection\label{a.3-pomdp-shape-match-across-all-23-targets}
\texttt{shape\_match} is a coarser invariant than ε: it asks, per axis,
whether both sides of the roundtrip have a non‑empty population for
\texttt{n\_states}, \texttt{n\_obs}, and \texttt{n\_actions}. Across all
23 targets, shape match is TRUE on every axis for which the origin had ≥
1 entry; the zoo fixtures 07--10 have \texttt{n\_states\ =\ 0} on the
origin and \texttt{n\_states\ =\ 1} on the synth because the synthesizer
always emits at least one hidden-state factor.
\end{block}
